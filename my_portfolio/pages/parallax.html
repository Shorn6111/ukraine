Russo-Ukrainian War

Background

Before the Fall of the Soviet Union, Russia and Ukraine were both states within the Union. The Soviet union also had formed the warsaw pact with many eastern European nations which gave the Soviets a buffer zone between them and their rival NATO. When the Warsaw Pact dissolved and the Soviet Union collapsed it lead to many former Soviet states to claim independence and break away. Ukraine was one of those states, They decided to become independent after the many atrocities they faced while under Soviet control. Russia in thge following years after the war slowly saw more and more allies abandon them and join Nato. In 2004 the first former Soviet republic joined Nato.
