History

Alaska is the largest state in the United States. We were the 49th state to be admitted into the the union. Our state capital is the great city of Junea. Before we became a state we were considered a territory by the US after we were purchaed by the United States from Russia. The US bought our land for $7.2 million US dollars or about two cents per acre. Alaska was considered a worthless tundra by the Russians but soon after they sold gold and oil were discovered. On top of our land being very plentiful we have some of the most breathtaking sites to see on the planet.
